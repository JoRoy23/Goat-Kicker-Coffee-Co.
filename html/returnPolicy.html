Return Policy

Our return policy allows purchased coffees to be returned/exchanged at any of our four locations for store credit within 30 days of purchase.

This extends to all Goat Kicker Coffee retail merchandise with original packaging with no visible signs of wear and a valid receipt. Discounted items are final sale and cannot be returned.
