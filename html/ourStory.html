Our Story

It started with one simple idea:
Make great coffee

We are coffee passionnate. Every decision we make about our products, our services and all we do in the community is rooted in one basic concept: Make coffee great again. It's what move us and motivates us and keep us excited.

When Goat Kicker began roasting coffee in 2010, our intent was to provide top-quality wholesale beans for other cafés. But once people began coming to us for coffee to brew on their own, we knew it was time to expand. A bigger warehouse and welcoming storefront allowed us to not only make great coffee for our customers, but also teach them how to brew it best themselves. Now, with four locations in Bali and multiple partnerships, we can share our passion with more people.

An Approachable Experience

Cappuccino or just plain coffee? By-the-cup single-origin or cold brew? Whether you’re a coffee connoisseur, new to the coffee-drinking experience or somewhere in between, we want everyone to find something they love. Expertly crafted coffee doesn’t have to feel exclusive.

Our staff is eager to make recommendations and explain the tools of the trade. We also provide tours and classes to further your coffee knowledge and help you brew the perfect cup at home. Stop in to sample our coffee of the day or our featured flavor.

The Legend Behind the Name

Legend has it that a herdsman named Kaldi noticed his goats had an extra spring in their step after eating the wild berries that grew near their feeding grounds. Curious, Kaldi tried the berries himself and experienced the same euphoria. The seeds of those berries are the coffee “bean” — which is why we can thank those goats for the delicious drink we have today.
